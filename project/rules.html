Rules

The rules of Tic-tac-toe are quite simple:

  1. Players take turns.
  2. For the first game, the player that goes first is random.
  3. The player that goes first alternates for future games.
  4. You must fill exactly one square per turn.
  5. You may only fill squares with your shape.
  6. You may only fill empty squares.
  7. You win if you get three-in-a-row.
  8. Your opponent wins if they get three-in-a-row.
  9. It's a draw if all squares are filled with no three-in-a-rows.
